FAQ

Common Questions

Will this work with my CRM?
Yes. It works with VinSolutions, DealerSocket, and most major dealer CRMs, along with tools like vAuto, DealerTrack, kDealer, and manufacturer portals.
How long does setup take?
Starter is typically delivered in 3-5 days. The middle package usually takes 7-10 days. The full buildout runs longer because it includes deeper setup and coaching.
Do I need technical staff to maintain this?
No. The system runs on a Mac or PC that stays on at your desk. After setup and the walkthrough, it should be easy to live with. Support is included in every package.
Can AI really automate my morning reports?
Yes. It can pull from VinSolutions, vAuto, manufacturer portals, and your desk log, then build the report and send it to your phone before the day starts. Read the full breakdown.
How much time will I actually save?
For most managers, the biggest gains come from reporting, CRM cleanup, email sorting, and desk work. It adds up fast, especially when those tasks were eating the first part of every day.
Can you set this up remotely?
Yes. Everything is done remotely by screen share, so location is not the issue. The main question is whether the workflow is worth fixing.
