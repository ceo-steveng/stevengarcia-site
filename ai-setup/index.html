Common Questions

Will this work with my CRM?
Works with VinSolutions, DealerSocket, and most major dealer CRMs. It connects through browser automation, so if you can log in and use it, the AI can too. Also integrates with vAuto, DealerTrack, kDealer, and manufacturer portals.
How long does setup take?
The Starter package is delivered in 3-5 days. Full Desk takes 7-10 days. Full Stack runs over 3 months. You'll have automations running within the first week regardless of package.
Do I need technical staff to maintain this?
No. The system runs on a Mac or PC that stays on at your desk. After the initial setup and walkthrough call, it runs autonomously. If something breaks, support is included in every package.
Can AI really automate my morning reports?
Yes. It pulls from VinSolutions, vAuto, manufacturer portals, and your desk log. Builds a PDF. Sends it to your phone before your alarm goes off. I've been running mine this way for months. Read the full breakdown.
How much time will I actually save?
Most managers save 2+ hours per day. Morning reports (45 min), CRM cleanup (30 min), email sorting (60 min), desk log (20 min). That's 500+ hours a year you get back to sell cars.
Can you set this up remotely?
Yes. Everything is done remotely via screen share. Doesn't matter if you're in San Antonio, Dallas, or Detroit. Same setup, same results.
